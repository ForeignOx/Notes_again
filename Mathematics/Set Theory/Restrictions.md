If $R$ is a [[Relations|relation]] and $A$ is any [[Sets|set]], then the restriction of $R$ to $A$, $R\restriction A$, is the [[Subsets|subset]] or $R$ containing those [[Ordered Pairs|pairs]] of elements with the first element in $A$:
$$
R\restriction A=\left\{ (x,y)\mid(x,y)\in R\text{ and }x\in  A \right\}=R\cap(A\times \text{range}(R))
$$
