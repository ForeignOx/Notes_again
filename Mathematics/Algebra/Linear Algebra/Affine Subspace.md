## Definition
For some [[Vectorspaces|vectorspace]] $V$, take some [[Subspaces|subspace]] $U$, then an affine subspace of $V$ can be defined to be
$$
\underline{a}+U=\left\{ \underline{a}+\underline{u}\mid \underline{u}\in  U \right\}
$$
For some $\underline{a}\in V$
