## Definition
If $\underline{\alpha}=(\alpha_{1},\dots,\alpha_{n})$ is an $n$-tuple of [[Vectors|vectors]] in a [[Vectorspaces|vectorspace]] $W$, let $L_{\underline{\alpha}}$ be the corresponding [[Linear Combinations|linear combination]] [[Functions|mapping]]
$$
\underline{x}\mapsto \sum_{i=1}^{n}x_{i}\alpha_{i}
$$
From $\mathbb{R}^{n}\to W$
Note that the $n$-tuple $\underline{\alpha}$ itself is an element of $W^{n}$ 
If $T$ is any [[Linear Maps|linear mapping]] from $\mathbb{R}^{n}\to W$, we shall call the $n$-tuple $\left\{ T(\delta^{i}) \right\}_{1}^{n}$ the skeleton of $T$
