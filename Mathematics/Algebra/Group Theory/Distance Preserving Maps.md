## Definition
Let $G$ be the [[Sets|set]] of all [[Functions|maps]] [[Vectorspace Rn|$\mathbb{R}^{n}\to \mathbb{R}^{n}$]]: [[Product Sets|$(\mathbb{R}^{n})^{\mathbb{R}^{n}}$]] preserving distances between the points
For $\underline{a},\underline{b}$:
$$
d(\underline{a},\underline{b})=\sqrt{ \sum_{ i=1} ^{ n}  (a_{i}-b_{i})^{2} }
$$
## Examples
Translations, rotations, reflections
## Remarks
- All elements of $G$ are [[Bijective Functions|bijections]]
- $G$ is a [[Groups|group]] under [[Composition of Functions|composition of maps]]
- $G$ acts on $\mathbb{R}^{n}$ via $(\forall g\in G)(\forall x\in X)(g*x=g(x))$
- $G$ acts on specific subsets of $\mathbb{R}^{n}$ e.g. lines, planes, circles, pentagons,... nicely
- The action of $G$ are [[Transitive Actions|transitive]] as for all $x_{1},x_{2},\dots,x_{n}\in \mathbb{R}^{n}$, $\exists$ a map taking $\underline{x}_{i}$ to $\underline{x}_{j}$ (e.g. translation by a vector $\left| \underline{x}_{i}-\underline{x}_{j} \right|$)
