## Proposition
If [[Functions|$f$]]  is [[Continuity|continuous]] on an [[open sets|open set]] $U$, then for any point $a$ there exist $\rho>0,k>0$ such that $\left| f(z) \right|\leq k$ on $B_{\rho}(a)$
In particular, if $f(a)\neq 0$, there exist $\rho>0,k>0,M>0$ such that
$$
k\geq \left| f(z) \right| \geq M>0\text{ on }B_{\rho}(a)
$$
