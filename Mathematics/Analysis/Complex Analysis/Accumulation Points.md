## Definition
Given a [[sets|set]] [[Complex Numbers|$S\subset \mathbb{C}$]], we say that a point $w\in \mathbb{C}$ is an accumulation point of $S$ if 
$$
\forall \rho>0,\exists z\in S\setminus \left\{ w \right\}:z\in B_{\rho}(w)
$$
## Remark
Accumulation points of $S$ need not lie in $S$
We can show $w\in \mathbb{C}$ is an accumulation point of $S$ iff it is the limit point of some convergent sequence of points in $S$
