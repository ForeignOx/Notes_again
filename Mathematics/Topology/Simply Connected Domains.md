## Definition
An [[open sets|open set]] $U$ is simply connected if for every [[Curves|closed contour]] in $U$ we have [[Winding Number|$I_{\gamma}(w)=0$]] for every $w\not\in U$
### Examples
We find that all starlike domains are simply connected.
But consider $\mathbb{C}^{*}$ this is not simply connected because $0\not\in \mathbb{C}^{*}$, but for $\gamma(t)=e^{ it }$,
$$
I_{\gamma}(0)=\oint_{\left| z \right| =1} \frac{1}{z}~dz=2\pi i\neq 0
$$
